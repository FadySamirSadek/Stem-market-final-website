STEM Market is an annual, one-day,
STEM-based entrepreneurship expo!
(STEM: Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics)

STEM Market aspires to achieve both its mission and its long-term vision through its three main pillars:
- Make It Real STEM Market connects young entrepreneurs who have innovative projects in the stages of prototyping with pioneer industrial companies from the same community. Eventually, the companies would offer technical or financial support for some of the best projects.
- Invest in Innovation STEM Market is an event where investors are able to work with innovative minds and invest in their industrial start-ups to help them prosper.
- Share the Experience STEM Market is an opportunity for people who are interested in innovation, STEM fields, and entrepreneurship to learn about other experiences that might help them develop their own mind-sets through meeting with pioneers in entrepreneurship. reserve ticket
